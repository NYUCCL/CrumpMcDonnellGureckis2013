Instructions

At first, the test phase may be difficult and you may have to guess for some or all of the items. That is ok. We are interested in how people gradually learn. As you are given more learning opportunities the task will become easier. If you concentrate it is possible to master the task. Please use only your memory to solve the task and do not use a pen or paper (or your computer) to take notes.

In order to finish the experiment, you must successfully complete two test phases at 100% accuracy. The two phases of the experiment (learning, test) will loop until you can get two test phases completely right in a row. Each individual will take more or less time, so don't worry if you don't get it right away. Just keep at it and eventually it will become easier.

If you still have not achieved this after 20 rounds of learning, we will terminate the experiment and you will still receive your remuneration.

When you're ready to continue, press Next.
