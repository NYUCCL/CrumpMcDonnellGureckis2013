Instructions

At first, the test phase may be difficult and you may have to guess for some or all of the items. That is ok. We are interested in how people gradually learn. As you are given more learning opportunities the task will become easier. If you find it difficult and you have to guess that is ok. Please use only your own mind to solve the task and do not use a pen or paper (or your computer) to take notes (this is a cognitive psychology experiment).

In order to finish the experiment, you must successfully complete two test phases at 100% accuracy. Different people will take more or less time, so don't worry if you don't get it right away. Just keep at it and eventually it will become easier.

If you still have not achieved 100% accuracy after 15 rounds of learning, we will terminate the experiment and you will still receive your remuneration. We will remind you how many blocks remain until the end after each block.

When you're ready to continue, press Next.
