Instructions

After using all of your credits, you will be tested on each of the eight items you have seen.

You will try your best to respond with the category label that you have seen previously. To give a response, you would click 'A' button for a square whose label is A. You would click the 'B' button if the square was originally labeled 'B'.

After giving eight responses, you will recieve feedback on how you responded and loop back to learning the same squares you have seen previously again. In order to finish the experiment, you must succesfully complete two test phases at 100% accuracy. (E.g., the best possible performance would be two blocks of label phases and test phases). The experiment will loop until you can get two test phases completely right in a row.
