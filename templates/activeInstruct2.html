Instructions

After using all of your credits, you will be tested on each of the eight items you have seen. The screen will look like this:

Which category does the object belong to?

In the test phase, try your best to respond with the category label that you have seen previously. To give a response, you would click the button corresponding to the label you think is associated with the square.

After labeling each of the eight stimuli, you will recieve feedback on how you responded and go back to the training session to refresh your memory.

In order to finish the experiment, you must succesfully complete two test phases at 100% accuracy. The experiment will loop until you can get two test phases completely right in a row.

When you feel you understand what you will be doing in the test phase, press Next to continue.
