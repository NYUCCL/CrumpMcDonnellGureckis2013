Instructions

After using all of your credits, you will move on the test phase. During each test phase you will be tested on each of the eight objects you have seen. The screen will look like this:

Which group does the object belong to?

Try your best to respond with the label or group name that you have seen previously associated with that particular object. To give a response, you simply click the button corresponding to the label you think is associated with the object. Try it!

At the end of the test phase, after labeling each of the eight stimuli, you will receive feedback on how well you did, and then you will go back to the learning phase to refresh your memory.

At first, the test phase may be difficult and you may have to guess for some or all of the items. That is ok. We are interested in how people slowly learn. As you are given more learning opportunities the task will become easier. Please use only your memory to solve the task and do not use pen and paper.

When you're ready to continue, press Next.
