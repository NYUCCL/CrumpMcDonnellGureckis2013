Instructions

After using all of your credits, you will move on the test phase. During each test phase you will be tested on objects presented individually. The screen will look something like this:

Which group does the object belong to?

In the test phase, try your best to respond with the correct label or group name for that particular object. To give a response, you simply click the button corresponding to the label you think is associated with the object. Try it out above!

At the end of the test phase, you will receive feedback on how well you did, and (if you have not yet mastered the grouping) then you will go back to the learning phase to continue your learning.

When you're ready to continue, press Next.
