Instructions

During the task, you will view object presented one at a time and you will be asked to judge the group membership. The screen will look something like this:

Which group does the object belong to?

Try your best to respond with the correct group name for that particular object. To give a response, you simply click the button corresponding to the label you think is associated with the object. Afterwards you will receive feedback about the correct membership. Try it out above!

The experiment is organized into rounds of 16 test trials. At the end of each round of trials, you will learn what your average score was. Then you will go on to another round where you will get to make guesses about the items again. Your objective is to get 100% correct in two blocks in a row. The rule will not change from round to round.

When you're ready to continue, press Next.
