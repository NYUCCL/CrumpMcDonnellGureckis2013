Instructions

During the task, you will be asked to make judgments about the labels of objects presented individually. The screen will look something like this:

Which group does the object belong to?

Try your best to respond with the correct label or group name for that particular object. To give a response, you simply click the button corresponding to the label you think is associated with the object. Try it out above!

Batches of test questions are organized into blocks of 16 test trials. At the end of the test block, you will learn what your average score was. Your objective is to get 100% correct in two blocks in a row.

When you're ready to continue, press Next.
