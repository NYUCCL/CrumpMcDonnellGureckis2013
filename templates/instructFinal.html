Instructions

Before starting the experiment, we would like to request that you try your best remove yourself from all possible sources of distraction.

We ask that you turn off any external noise source (e.g. music, television), close all other windows open on your computer, ask other people to leave your immediate environment or to not distract you, and to concentrate on the experiment at hand. Evidence that you have not followed these instructions (excessively long response times) could lead to revocation of payment.

Most importantly, please do not use any assistive aids such as writing or drawing to help you remember. We are interested in how people learn using only their memory. Please try to remember which group name goes which each object on your own. It may be difficult at first but will get easier as you go.

To review:

You will be learning whether the square objects belong to either group A or B over the course of several sessions. After each session, you will be tested on your knowledge for the labels. To complete the task, you must get a perfect score two sessions in a row.

When you're ready, click the "Start" button to begin!
