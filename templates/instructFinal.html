Instructions

Before starting the experiment, we would like to request that you try your best remove all possible sources of distraction.

Please turn off any external noise source (e.g. music, television), close all other windows open on your computer, ask other people to leave your immediate environment or to not distract you, and to concentrate on the experiment at hand. Evidence that you have not followed these instructions (for example, excessively long response times) could lead to revocation of payment.

Most importantly, please do not use any writing or drawings (or your computer) to help you remember. We are cognitive psychologists interested in how people learn using only their memory. We rely on your honesty for this. Please try to remember which group name goes which each object on your own. While the task may be difficult at first, it will get easier as you go, and it is possible to master the groups after a few minutes.

To review:

You will be learning whether the square objects belong to either group A or B over the course of several sessions. After each session, you will be tested on your knowledge for the labels. To complete the task, you must get a perfect score two sessions in a row.

When you're ready, click the "Start" button to begin!
