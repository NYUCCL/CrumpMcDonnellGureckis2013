The Cognition and Computation Lab at New York University (NYU) is looking for online participants for a brief experiment in cognitive psychology. The experiment can be run right at your desk. The only formal requirements are that you are at least 18 years old and are a fluent English speaker. The experiment will take anywhere between 10 to 20 minutes to complete and you will be remunerated $1.00 for your time.
