Instructions

Due to the online nature of this experiment, we want to best simulate a lab-like setting. We would like to request that you best remove yourself from all distraction.

We ask that you turn off any external noise source (e.g. music, television), close all other windows open on your computer, ask other people to leave your immediate environment or to not distract you, and to simply concentrate on the experiment at hand. Evidence that you have not followed these instructions could lead to revocation of payment.

Most importantly, you may not use any assisitive learning aids such as writing down or drawing labels to help you remember. You must pay enough attention so you will be able to remember the labels on your own.

To review:

You will learning whether the squares belong to 'A' or 'B' over the course of several sessions. After each session, you will be tested on your knowledge for the labels. To complete the task, you must get a perfect score two sessions in a row.
